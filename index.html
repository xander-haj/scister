OCR Image Processor with Text Highlighting

Upload an image and perform OCR. The recognized words will be highlighted on the image. Click on a highlighted word to assign it to one of the input fields. You can edit fields manually as well. Once done, press "Update Table" to add a row to the table. You can process multiple images, clearing and adding new rows without reloading the page. Finally, press "Save CSV" to download all collected data.
